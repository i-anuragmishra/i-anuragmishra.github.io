About Me

I'm Anurag Mishra, a dedicated AI researcher and engineer with a Master's degree in Artificial Intelligence from RIT. My journey in AI is driven by a deep curiosity to understand and advance the field of machine learning, particularly in explainable AI and ethical AI development. With expertise in natural language processing, deep learning, and computer vision, I've worked on groundbreaking projects in deepfake detection and bias mitigation in large language models.

Beyond research, I've honed my leadership and organizational skills as the Secretary of the RIT AI Club, where I've fostered collaboration, hosted events, and driven discussions around cutting-edge AI topics. My work focuses on developing robust AI systems that combine state-of-the-art performance with interpretability and ethical considerations.

I am passionate about building AI systems that are not only innovative but also ethical, fair, and practical. Whether it's exploring the nuances of neural machine translation, developing explainable AI systems, or investigating adversarial robustness in deep learning models, I thrive on bridging the gap between theoretical research and real-world applications. I'm always eager to tackle new challenges and contribute to advancing the field of AI.
